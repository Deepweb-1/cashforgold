FAQS

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do you pay more than my local pawn and jewelry shops?

CashForGoldCanada.com is a web operated business which means we have an extremely low overhead compared to a traditional brick and mortar business. Also, because of our large purchasing volume we are able to pass these volume discounts on to our valued clients, which means a better price for your gold.

Is the GoldExpress Kit package free?

Yes. The GoldExpress Kit is absolutely free upon your request. There is no obligation once you have received your kit but if you do decide not to use it, we would greatly appreciate you passing it on to someone else or sending the kit back to us by writing “Return To Sender” on the envelope and dropping it in any mailbox. This helps us to reduce expenses and keep our payouts as high as possible for our clients.

Can I send you my gold WITHOUT the free GoldExpress Kit?

Yes! If you would prefer to send your gold to us without waiting to receive the GoldExpress Kit, you may do so. Please click here to read the instructions.

How do I use the GoldExpress Kit once I receive it?

Your kit will arrive at the address you have provided in a normal business envelope. It will come with the following components:

  • Your custom, bar coded, trackable and insured* Canada XpressPost™ envelope as well as a tracking receipt and our mailing address pre-addressed on the package.
  • A thick (high quality) padded bag to safely hold your scrap gold items.
  • A personal letter from us giving you instructions on how to pack your gold items.
  • An informational payment form for you to fill out and return back to us with your scrap gold

All you will need to do is fill out our payment form, put your gold in the padded envelope, place everything into the Canada ExpressPost envelope and put it in the mail! Postage and insurance are pre-paid by us.

Is my GoldExpress Kit insured if it is lost in the mail?

Ship with confidence!
Every regular Gold Kit Express shipment includes insurance coverage of up to $500 CAD, with high value kit options available for shipments requiring coverage of up to $5,000 CAD. Please email us for insurance over $5,000. 

This coverage applies to qualifying packages that are lost in transit and do not arrive to us. It does not extend to claims relating to the contents of a package after shipment, such as missing items, incomplete contents, empty parcels, or suspected tampering. This policy helps us maintain a secure and reliable service for all customers.

Important
Insurance applies only when the kit is shipped using the instructions, packaging, and shipping method provided by Cash for Gold Canada. If you believe your shipment may exceed the standard coverage amount, please email us before mailing your items so we can help arrange a higher value kit.

How can I be sure I will get paid for the items I send?

CashForGoldCanada.com has paid out millions of dollars to thousands of satisfied clients. Our number one priority is customer satisfaction. In this business, a good reputation is imperative, which is why we work hard to establish trusting relationships with each and every one of our valued clients. We are also an “A ” rated member of the BBB (Better Business Bureau). You can be ensured you will be paid top dollar for your scrap gold.
